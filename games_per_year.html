Games Per Year

This page allows you to explore the number of international soccer games per year! Notice how the number of games has steadily grown through the history of the sport, with over 4000 international contests annually in recent years. Use the dropdown to explore the soccer history of a specific nation.

Tip: By default, this chart shows the number of games for all teams, but it can also be explored further! Use the dropdown to pick a specific team and learn about their history of soccer matched per year.

Tip: This chart includes tooltips! Hovever over a datapoint to learn how many games a specific national teams played that year.
