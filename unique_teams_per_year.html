This page allows you to explore the evolution of international soccer through the years - from a burgeoning game to a global spectacle! From the first game in 1872 between England and Scotland, soccer has grown into the most popular sport in the world. That can best be personified by the number of countries field teams each year. This chart allows you to explore the evolution of the sport through the lense of growing the game to new locations.
